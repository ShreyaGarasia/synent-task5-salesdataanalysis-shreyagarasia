# Task5 - Sales Analysis

## Problem Statement
Analyze the Superstore Sales Dataset to evaluate business performance, identify revenue trends, determine top-selling products, and analyze profitability.

## Objective
Evaluate business performance through sales and profit analysis.

## Dataset Details

| Attribute | Value |
|------------|--------|
| Dataset | Superstore Sales Dataset |
| Domain | Business Analytics |
| Records | 51,290 |
| Features | Sales, Profit, Product, Category, Region, Discount, Order Date |

## Approach
- Analyzed monthly revenue trends
- Identified top-selling products
- Evaluated category-wise and region-wise profit
- Examined discount impact on profitability

## Results
- Revenue trends successfully identified
- Top-performing products highlighted
- Profitability analyzed across categories and regions

## Key Insights
- Revenue varies across different months and seasons..
- Higher discounts may negatively impact profit margins.
