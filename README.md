# Task5 - Sales Analysis

## Problem Statement

Analyze the Superstore Sales Dataset to evaluate business performance, identify revenue trends, determine top-selling products, and analyze profitability.

## Objective

Evaluate business performance through sales and profit analysis.

## Dataset Details

* **Dataset:** Superstore Orders Dataset(kaggle)
* **Domain:** Business Analytics
* **Records:** 51,290
* **Features:** Sales, Profit, Product, Category, Region, Discount, Order Date

## Approach

* Analyzed monthly revenue trends
* Identified top-selling products
* Evaluated category-wise profit
* Evaluated region-wise profit
* Examined the impact of discounts on profitability

## Results

* Successfully identified monthly revenue trends
* Determined top-performing products
* Analyzed profitability across categories and regions
* Generated business performance insights

## Key Insights

* Revenue varies across different months and seasons.
* Higher discounts may reduce profit margins.
